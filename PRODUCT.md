# Tive Strength OS - Product Vision

## Purpose
Tive is a high-performance strength training OS designed for serious lifters. It provides a "Scientific PWA" experience that handles complex load management, recovery tracking (ACWR), and physiological progression without the clutter of traditional fitness apps.

## Target Audience
- **The Data-Driven Lifter:** Values precision, logs every set, and monitors recovery metrics.
- **The Performance Athlete:** Uses scientific formulas (1RM, MEV-MAV-MRV) to optimize training.
- **The Minimalist:** Despises bloat and social-first fitness apps; wants a tool that gets out of the way.

## Brand & Tone
- **Professional & Precise:** Every element should feel engineered and purposeful.
- **Restrained Power:** Minimalist aesthetic that hides deep complexity.
- **Immersive:** Dark mode as the primary environment to minimize distraction.

## Strategic Principles
- **Speed to Log:** Logging a set should be the fastest action in the app.
- **Data over Decoration:** Visualizations should be clear and actionable, not just pretty.
- **Offline-First:** Must work flawlessly in gym basements with poor connectivity.

## Design Constraints
- **Color Strategy:** Restrained. Tinted Zinc neutrals (#09090b) with high-contrast primary accents (#ffffff).
- **Typography:** Modern grotesque or geometric sans (Inter/Outfit). Mono for numerical data.
- **Z-Index Scale:** Semantic (raised: 10, modal: 100, toast: 200, debug: 9999).
